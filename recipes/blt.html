BLT

Description

This a recipe for a classic sandwich, the BLT. Not many people know what BLT stands for, but it's Bacon, Lettuce, and Tomato! The three of these combine, with a spot of mayo, to make a seriously good sandwich. Follow along to learn how to make your first BLT!

Ingredients

  • Bread - 2 slices
  • Bacon - 4 Strips
  • Lettuce - 1 leaf
  • Tomato - 2 slices
  • Mayo
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Once preheated place your bacon on a pan and put in oven for 20 minutes. Remove and let cool on a paper towel.
  3. Put both slices of bread in a toaster.
  4. Once toasted remove and use a butter knife to spread mayo on one side of each slice.
  5. On "bottom" slice of bread place leaf of lettuce
  6. On top of lettuce leaf place tomato slices.
  7. On top of tomato put strips of bacon.
  8. Season with salt and pepper, to tast
  9. Cover with "top" slice of bread. cut in half, and enjoy!
