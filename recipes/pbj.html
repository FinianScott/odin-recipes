PB&J

Description

It's a little know fact but PB & J stands for Peanut Butter and Jelly! Have you ever had one? I have and I have to say they are pretty damn tasty. Follow the recipe below to make one yourself.

Ingredients

  • Bread - 2 slices
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly - your flavor of choice

Steps

  1. Grab your two slices of bread and put them on a plate. For an optimal PB&J experience toast the bread first.
  2. Grab your peanut butter and spread a thick layer over one slice of bread.
  3. Grab you jelly and spread it across the other slice of bread
  4. Put both pieces of bread together peanut butter facing jelly. Cut in half and enjoy!
